ANGEA

Pangea is a group dedicated to environmental preservation and sustainability. Our mission is to give knowledge and awareness to Earthlings in the fight against climate change, pollution and other factors that can destroy our environment. We believe that being aware and taking action against these factors, we can create a healthier, more sustainable future for our planet.
